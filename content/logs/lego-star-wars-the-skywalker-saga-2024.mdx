## The Feel

LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga on Nintendo Switch is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> FORCE SENSITIVITY: Detected. A great disturbance in the Force.

## The Design

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "I don't like sand" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
