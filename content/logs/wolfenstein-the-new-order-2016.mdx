## The Setup

Wolfenstein: The New Order lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> OPERATOR LOG: Mecha.

## The Gunplay

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Mecha".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Wolfenstein: The New Order doesn't waste your time.
