## The World

Prototype 2 on PS3 is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> INFECTED ZONE: Mercer consuming biomass. Level rising.

## The Combat

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "was a bit boring" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Prototype 2 is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
