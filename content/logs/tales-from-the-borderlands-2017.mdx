## The Story

Tales from the Borderlands is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PC and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> ATLAS PROTOCOL: Fiona and Rhys. The vault hunters nobody asked for.

## The System

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Come with me if you want to leave" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Tales from the Borderlands earns its reputation. Play it.
