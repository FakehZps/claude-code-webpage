## The Experience

Deus Ex: Human Revolution - Director's Cut lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> AUGMENTATION ONLINE: I never asked for this.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Lost Augs half way through but recovered before the end".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Deus Ex: Human Revolution - Director's Cut doesn't waste your time.
