## The World

Shadow of the Colossus lands on PS4 (IGC) with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> DORMIN SPEAKS: Thou art a skilled warrior. The price is steep.

## The Combat

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. The polish shows throughout.

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Shadow of the Colossus doesn't waste your time.
