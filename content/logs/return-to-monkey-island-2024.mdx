## The Experience

Return to Monkey Island on Steam Deck (Xbox Game Pass) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> ITEM OBTAINED: Rubber chicken with a pulley in the middle.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "Worst nothing ending we'd already seen in MI2" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Return to Monkey Island has potential it never fulfils.
