## The Story

Xenoblade Chronicles 3 on Nintendo Switch sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> VISION DETECTED: Future threat identified. Shulk, be on guard.

## The System

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Hear that Noah? Lanz wants something a bit meatier" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Xenoblade Chronicles 3 does its job without distinction.
