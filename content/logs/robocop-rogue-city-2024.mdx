## The Experience

Robocop: Rogue City on Playstation 5 is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> LAW ENFORCEMENT PROTOCOL: Directive 4 classified. Crime: 0%.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "There will be trouble" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Robocop: Rogue City has potential it never fulfils.
