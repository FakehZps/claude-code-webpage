## The World

Assassin's Creed II lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> EAGLE VISION: Target synchronised. Memory sequence unlocked.

## The Combat

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "it's a me Mario".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Assassin's Creed II doesn't waste your time.
