## The World

God of War is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PS4 (Physical) and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> SPARTAN RAGE: Charging. Atreus, stay close.

## The Combat

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Plat'd" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. God of War earns its reputation. Play it.
