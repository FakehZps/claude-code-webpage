## The Feel

Tetris Effect: Connected is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PC (Xbox Gamepass) and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> LINE CLEARED: Tetrimino sequence optimal. Journey mode active.

## The Design

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Journey - dropped to easy on last stage" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Tetris Effect: Connected earns its reputation. Play it.
