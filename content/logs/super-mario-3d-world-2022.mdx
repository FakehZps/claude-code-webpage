## The Feel

Super Mario 3D World on Switch (Physical) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> STAR POWER: Collected. Bowser won't know what hit him.

## The Design

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "not including Bowsers Fury" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Super Mario 3D World does its job without distinction.
