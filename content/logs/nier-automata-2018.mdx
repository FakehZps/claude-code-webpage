## The Story

Nier Automata on PS4 is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> RESOURCE LOG: Seeking the promised land. Glory to mankind.

## The System

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "For the glory of mankind" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Nier Automata is exactly what it needs to be.
