## The Track

Wipeout Omega Collection on PS4 (IGC) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> OPERATOR LOG: Did all races.

## The Feel

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "did all races" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Wipeout Omega Collection has potential it never fulfils.
