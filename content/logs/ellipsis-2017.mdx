## The Experience

Ellipsis on PC is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: Dots.

## The Detail

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "dots" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Ellipsis is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
