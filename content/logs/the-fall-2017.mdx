## The Experience

The Fall on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: You are faulty.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "You are faulty" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. The Fall does its job without distinction.
