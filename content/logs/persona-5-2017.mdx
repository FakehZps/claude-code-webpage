## The Story

Persona 5 is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PS4 and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> SOCIAL LINK RANK UP: Bond strengthened.

## The System

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Masks" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Persona 5 earns its reputation. Play it.
