## The Experience

Frozen Synapse Prime lands on Vita with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> SIMULATION: All units locked and loaded. Execute order now.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "good music, good shapeforms".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Frozen Synapse Prime doesn't waste your time.
