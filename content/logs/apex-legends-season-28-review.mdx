## Still Here

Two seasons in a row. That hasn't happened in a while. Season 28 maintains the ranked improvements from 27 and iterates — new legend ability tweaks, a care package rotation that actually changes high-level play, and a map update that opens up previously stagnant rotations.

> CHAMPION SQUAD: Eliminated. Cause of death: Coil. Obviously.

## What Changed

The ring changes in Season 28 are the most significant in three seasons. Late-game circles that previously funnelled into predictable positions now have genuine variance. High-ground advantages are less deterministic. The final ten are more chaotic and, paradoxically, more skill-rewarding.

The legend meta is healthier than it's been since the support class overhaul. Three viable archetypes in ranked rather than one dominant pick.

> BANGALORE: Smoke deployed. Vision: zero. Plan: unclear.

## The Constant

Coil is still doing it. You know what he's doing. You've seen the kill feed. The third-party from the building everyone agreed not to push from. The ambush on the team that just finished a fight. The inexplicable decision to split from the squad at ring closure.

It is, somehow, still affectionate.

## Final Verdict

*Still Coil's fault.* Season 28 is the game at a consistent high. The Outlands have earned another season of my time.
