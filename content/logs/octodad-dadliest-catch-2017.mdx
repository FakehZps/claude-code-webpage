## The Experience

Octodad: Dadliest Catch on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: That explains a lot.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "That explains a lot" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Octodad: Dadliest Catch does its job without distinction.
