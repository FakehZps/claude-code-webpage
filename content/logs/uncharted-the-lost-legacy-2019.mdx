## The World

Uncharted: The Lost Legacy lands on PS4 with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> FORTUNE HUNTER LOG: Treasure located. Also someone is shooting at me.

## The Combat

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. The polish shows throughout.

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Uncharted: The Lost Legacy doesn't waste your time.
