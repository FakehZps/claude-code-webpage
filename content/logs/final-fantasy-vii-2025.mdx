## The Story

Final Fantasy VII is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on Nintendo Switch (Physical) and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> BATTLE SYSTEM: ATB gauge full. Limit break available.

## The System

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Flower girl!" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Final Fantasy VII earns its reputation. Play it.
