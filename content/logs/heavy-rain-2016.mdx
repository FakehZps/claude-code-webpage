## The Premise

Heavy Rain on PS3 is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> ORIGAMI KILLER: Another victim found. Time is running out.

## The Choices

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "Shaun!" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Heavy Rain is exactly what it needs to be.
