## The Feel

Mario + Rabbids Kingdom Battle on Switch (Physical) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> STAR POWER: Collected. Bowser won't know what hit him.

## The Design

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Opera boss best boss" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Mario + Rabbids Kingdom Battle is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
