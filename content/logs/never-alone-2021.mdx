## The Experience

Never Alone on PS4 (IGC) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> SPIRIT WORLD: Nuna and Fox must work together.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Rolled right Inuit" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Never Alone does its job without distinction.
