## The World

Batman Arkham Origins on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> DETECTIVE MODE: Three suspects. One truth.

## The Combat

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "I'm Batman!" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Batman Arkham Origins is exactly what it needs to be.
