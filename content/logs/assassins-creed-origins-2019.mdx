## The World

Assassin's Creed Origins on PS4 is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> EAGLE VISION: Target synchronised. Memory sequence unlocked.

## The Combat

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. It won't blow your mind, but it won't waste your time either.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Assassin's Creed Origins is exactly what it needs to be.
