## The Experience

Catherine: Full Body on Nintendo Switch (Physical) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> NIGHTMARE BLOCK: Level 20. Vincent's conscience weighs heavily.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "Aliens?" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Catherine: Full Body has potential it never fulfils.
