## The Experience

Abzû on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> DIVE LOG: Depth 200m. Bioluminescent entities detected.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "Under the sea" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Abzû is exactly what it needs to be.
