## The Experience

Fifa 19 on PC (Origin) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> SYSTEM LOG: Entry filed. Fifa 19 — mission complete.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Fifa 19 does its job without distinction.
