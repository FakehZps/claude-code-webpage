## The Premise

Virtue's Last Reward on Vita is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: Platinum.

## The Choices

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Platinum" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Virtue's Last Reward is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
