## The Story

Final Fantasy I on Nintendo Switch (Physical) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> BATTLE SYSTEM: ATB gauge full. Limit break available.

## The System

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "a bit dated" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Final Fantasy I has potential it never fulfils.
