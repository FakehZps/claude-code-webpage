## The Feel

Bayonetta lands on Wii U with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> WITCH TIME: Active. Umbra witches do not forgive.

## The Design

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Completed on xbox and wii u".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Bayonetta doesn't waste your time.
