## The Experience

Deus Ex: Mankind Divided on PS4 (IGC) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> AUGMENTATION ONLINE: I never asked for this.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Augs!" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Deus Ex: Mankind Divided does its job without distinction.
