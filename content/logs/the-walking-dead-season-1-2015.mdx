## The Premise

The Walking Dead: Season 1 is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PC and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> SURVIVAL LOG: Day 18. Group morale critical.

## The Choices

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Completed original season 10/11/2014, completed dlc 29/01/2015" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. The Walking Dead: Season 1 earns its reputation. Play it.
