## The Story

Final Fantasy XIII-2 on PC is a difficult recommendation. Despite some promising ideas, the experience consistently frustrates more than it entertains. There are better options in almost every direction.

> BATTLE SYSTEM: ATB gauge full. Limit break available.

## The System

The core problems are fundamental — not patching, not preference. "zzzzzzz" pretty much nails it. Some things work in isolation, but they can't carry the whole.

## Final Verdict

Hard to recommend. Final Fantasy XIII-2 is for completionists only.
