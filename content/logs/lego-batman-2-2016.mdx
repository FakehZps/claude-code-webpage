## The World

Lego Batman 2 on PC is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> DETECTIVE MODE: Three suspects. One truth.

## The Combat

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Help" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Lego Batman 2 is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
