## The Setup

Sniper Elite 4 on PS4 (IGC) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: Nazi's.

## The Gunplay

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Nazi's" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Sniper Elite 4 is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
