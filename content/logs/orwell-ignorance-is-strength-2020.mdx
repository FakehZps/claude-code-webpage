## The Experience

Orwell: Ignorance is Strength on PC (Origin) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: Big Brother.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Big Brother" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Orwell: Ignorance is Strength does its job without distinction.
