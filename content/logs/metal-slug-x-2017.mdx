## The Setup

Metal Slug X on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: OK!.

## The Gunplay

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "OK!" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Metal Slug X does its job without distinction.
