## The World

Assassin's Creed Rogue is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PS3 and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> EAGLE VISION: Target synchronised. Memory sequence unlocked.

## The Combat

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Almost 100%ed it" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Assassin's Creed Rogue earns its reputation. Play it.
