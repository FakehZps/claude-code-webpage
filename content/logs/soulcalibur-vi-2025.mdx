## The Experience

Soulcalibur VI on Playstation 4 (Physical) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> CURSED SWORD: Soul Edge detected. Nightmare approaches.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "The legend died" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Soulcalibur VI has potential it never fulfils.
