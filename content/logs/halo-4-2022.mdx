## The Setup

Halo 4 on PC (xbox Gamepass) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> UNSC PRIORITY TRANSMISSION: Chief, the Covenant are closing in.

## The Gunplay

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Co-op with Relentless and Mixa part of MCC" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Halo 4 is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
