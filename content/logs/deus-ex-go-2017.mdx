## The Experience

Deus Ex GO on Android sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> AUGMENTATION ONLINE: I never asked for this.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Deus Ex GO does its job without distinction.
