## The Experience

Inside on PC (Origin) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> EXPERIMENT STATUS: Subject acquired. Facility alert.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "The blob" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Inside does its job without distinction.
