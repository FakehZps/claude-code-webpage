## The Story

New Tales from the Borderlands on Nintendo Switch is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> ATLAS PROTOCOL: Fiona and Rhys. The vault hunters nobody asked for.

## The System

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "Vaultlanders!" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. New Tales from the Borderlands has potential it never fulfils.
