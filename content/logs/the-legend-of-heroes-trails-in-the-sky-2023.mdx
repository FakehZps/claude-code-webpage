## The Story

The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky lands on PC (Steam) with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> ORBAL ARTS LEARNED: Jenis Royal Academy awaits, Estelle.

## The System

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "It was the professor!".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky doesn't waste your time.
