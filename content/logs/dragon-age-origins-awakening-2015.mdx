## The Experience

Dragon Age: Origins - Awakening on PC is a difficult recommendation. Despite some promising ideas, the experience consistently frustrates more than it entertains. There are better options in almost every direction.

> WARDEN PROTOCOL: Darkspawn detected. The Blight must end.

## The Detail

The core problems are fundamental — not patching, not preference. "Got to final mission but bug meant could not continue" pretty much nails it. Some things work in isolation, but they can't carry the whole.

## Final Verdict

Hard to recommend. Dragon Age: Origins - Awakening is for completionists only.
