## The Premise

The Walking Dead: Michonne on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> SURVIVAL LOG: Day 115. Group morale critical.

## The Choices

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. It won't blow your mind, but it won't waste your time either.

## Final Verdict

Solid. The Walking Dead: Michonne is exactly what it needs to be.
