## The Story

Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Torna ~ The Golden Country on Switch (Physical) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> VISION DETECTED: Future threat identified. Shulk, be on guard.

## The System

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Better than the base game" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Torna ~ The Golden Country does its job without distinction.
