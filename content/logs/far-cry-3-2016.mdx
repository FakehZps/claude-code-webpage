## The Experience

Far Cry 3 lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> OUTPOST LIBERATED: Area secure. For now.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Knife fights".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Far Cry 3 doesn't waste your time.
