## The Story

Tales from the Borderlands lands on PS4 (IGC) with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> ATLAS PROTOCOL: Fiona and Rhys. The vault hunters nobody asked for.

## The System

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Finger guns".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Tales from the Borderlands doesn't waste your time.
