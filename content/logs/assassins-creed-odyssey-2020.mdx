## The World

Assassin's Creed Odyssey on PC (Uplay) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> EAGLE VISION: Target synchronised. Memory sequence unlocked.

## The Combat

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Malakas!" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Assassin's Creed Odyssey is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
