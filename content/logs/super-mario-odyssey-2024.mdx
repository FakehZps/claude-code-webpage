## The Feel

Super Mario Odyssey lands on Nintendo Switch with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> STAR POWER: Collected. Bowser won't know what hit him.

## The Design

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "waah".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Super Mario Odyssey doesn't waste your time.
