## The Experience

Open Sorcery on PC is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> SYSTEM LOG: Entry filed. Open Sorcery — mission complete.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. Play something else first.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Open Sorcery has potential it never fulfils.
