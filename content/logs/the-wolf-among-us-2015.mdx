## The Premise

The Wolf Among us lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> FABLETOWN CASE FILE: Bigby Wolf investigating. Glamour required.

## The Choices

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Once upon a time".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. The Wolf Among us doesn't waste your time.
