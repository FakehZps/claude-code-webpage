## The Experience

Metal Gear Solid V on PS4 (IGC) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> CODEC CALL: Kept you waiting, huh?

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "completed the 31 story missions, no idea what was going on" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Metal Gear Solid V does its job without distinction.
