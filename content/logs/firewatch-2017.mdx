## The Premise

Firewatch on PC is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> DISPATCH: Delilah, I think I found something. Over.

## The Choices

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. Worth a playthrough if the genre appeals.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Firewatch is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
