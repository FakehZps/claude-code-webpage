## The Experience

The Witcher 2 on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> CONTRACT ACCEPTED: Monster sighted. Coin not guaranteed.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. The Witcher 2 does its job without distinction.
