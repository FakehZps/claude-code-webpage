Sam & Max: The Devil's Playhouse on PC is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "The end" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Sam & Max: The Devil's Playhouse has potential it never fulfils.
