## The Experience

Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> OUTPOST LIBERATED: Area secure. For now.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "If guns make me safe, bigger guns make me safer" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon is exactly what it needs to be.
