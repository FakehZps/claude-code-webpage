## The Experience

A Plague Tale: Requiem on PC (Xbox Game Pass) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> SWARM PROXIMITY: Hugo, stay close. Do not touch the rats.

## The Detail

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "It happens again" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. A Plague Tale: Requiem is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
