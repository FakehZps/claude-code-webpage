## The Experience

Deadlight lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> OPERATOR LOG: Ratman.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Ratman".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Deadlight doesn't waste your time.
