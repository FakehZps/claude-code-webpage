## The Feel

Bayonetta 2 is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on Wii U and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> WITCH TIME: Active. Umbra witches do not forgive.

## The Design

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "Another annoying kid in a game but game is good" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Bayonetta 2 earns its reputation. Play it.
