## The Experience

Saints Row IV on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> SAINTS HEADQUARTERS: Burt Reynolds just called. He wants his game back.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "you got the touch" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Saints Row IV is exactly what it needs to be.
