## The Story

Final Fantasy XIV: Stormblood lands on PC (Steam) with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> BATTLE SYSTEM: ATB gauge full. Limit break available.

## The System

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Mara Nyx Machinist".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Final Fantasy XIV: Stormblood doesn't waste your time.
