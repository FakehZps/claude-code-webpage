## The Experience

Shenmue on PS4 sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> INVESTIGATION LOG: The sailors. Always the sailors.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Where these sailers at?" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Shenmue does its job without distinction.
