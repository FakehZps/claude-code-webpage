## The Experience

Guacamelee lands on Vita with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> LUCHADOR STATUS: Mask equipped. Skeleton army defeated.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "What's in Mexico?".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Guacamelee doesn't waste your time.
