## The Experience

Beneath a Steel Sky on PC (GOG) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> UPLINK SECURED: Foster is back. The Union City Underground lives.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "Foster" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Beneath a Steel Sky has potential it never fulfils.
