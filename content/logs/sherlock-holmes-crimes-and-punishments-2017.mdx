## The Experience

Sherlock Holmes: Crimes and Punishments on PS3 sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> SYSTEM LOG: Entry filed. Sherlock Holmes: Crimes and Punishments — mission complete.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Sherlock Holmes: Crimes and Punishments does its job without distinction.
