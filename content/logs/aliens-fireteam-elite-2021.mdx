## The Experience

Aliens: Fireteam Elite on PC (Xbox Gamepass) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: Co-op'd.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Co-op'd" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Aliens: Fireteam Elite does its job without distinction.
