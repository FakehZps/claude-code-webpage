## The Atmosphere

Alan Wake 2 on Playstation 5 (Physical) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> MANUSCRIPT PAGE: The darkness is real. Write the ending.

## The Fear

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Paid a heavy price" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Alan Wake 2 is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
