## The Track

Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit on PC is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: Nee naw.

## The Feel

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "nee naw" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
