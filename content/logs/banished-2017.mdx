## The Hook

Banished on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: 100% achievements.

## The Depth

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "100% achievements" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Banished is exactly what it needs to be.
