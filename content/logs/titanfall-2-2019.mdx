## The Setup

Titanfall 2 lands on PC (Origin) with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> TITAN READY: BT-7274 online. Protocol 3 engaged.

## The Gunplay

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. The polish shows throughout.

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Titanfall 2 doesn't waste your time.
