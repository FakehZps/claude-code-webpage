## The Story

Shin Megami Tensei V on Switch (Physical) is a difficult recommendation. Despite some promising ideas, the experience consistently frustrates more than it entertains. There are better options in almost every direction.

> OPERATOR LOG: Destroyed the throne (neutral ending).

## The System

The core problems are fundamental — not patching, not preference. "Destroyed the throne (neutral ending)" pretty much nails it. Some things work in isolation, but they can't carry the whole.

## Final Verdict

Hard to recommend. Shin Megami Tensei V is for completionists only.
