## The Experience

The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim - Special Edition lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> SHOUT DETECTED: Fus Ro Dah. Enemies scattered.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Fo Rus fgdg..top of each guild...defeated Alduin".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim - Special Edition doesn't waste your time.
