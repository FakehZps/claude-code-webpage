## The Experience

Driver San Francisco lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> SHIFT DETECTED: Jericho located. The chase is on.

## The Detail

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Jericho!".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Driver San Francisco doesn't waste your time.
