## The Premise

Everybody's Gone to the Rapture on PS4 sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: Walking sim.

## The Choices

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Walking sim" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Everybody's Gone to the Rapture does its job without distinction.
