## The Experience

Warhammer 40,000 Space Marine on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> BATTLE REPORT: Bolter rounds expended: 4,000. Heretics purged: countless.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "For the God Emperor!" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Warhammer 40,000 Space Marine is exactly what it needs to be.
