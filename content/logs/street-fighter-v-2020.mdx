## The Experience

Street Fighter V on PS4 (IGC) is a frustrating experience — not bad enough to stop, but not good enough to enjoy. The foundations are there, but the execution lets the whole thing down.

> OPERATOR LOG: Just did all story stuff.

## The Detail

Every good idea seems countered by a bad decision. The pacing drags, the systems feel incomplete, and the moments of genuine fun are too infrequent. "just did all story stuff" — that's the kindest thing that can be said.

## Final Verdict

Disappointing. Street Fighter V has potential it never fulfils.
