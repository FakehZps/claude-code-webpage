## The Experience

South Park: The Stick of Truth on PC is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> AUTHORITY: Warning. This game contains mature content. A lot of it.

## The Detail

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "don't fart on a man's balls" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. South Park: The Stick of Truth is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
