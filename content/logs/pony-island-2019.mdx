## The Experience

Pony Island on PC (Steam) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> ERROR: Leviathan is experiencing a slight malfunction.

## The Detail

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. Worth a playthrough if the genre appeals.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Pony Island is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
