## The World

Uncharted 2: Among Thieves Remastered is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PS4 and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> FORTUNE HUNTER LOG: Treasure located. Also someone is shooting at me.

## The Combat

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. "A 4" captures it well — that's exactly how it feels to play.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Uncharted 2: Among Thieves Remastered earns its reputation. Play it.
