## The Premise

The Walking Dead: Season 2 lands on PC with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> SURVIVAL LOG: Day 175. Group morale critical.

## The Choices

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "Alone".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. The Walking Dead: Season 2 doesn't waste your time.
