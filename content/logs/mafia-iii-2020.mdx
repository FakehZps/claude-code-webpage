## The Experience

Mafia III on PS4 (IGC) is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> FAMILY BUSINESS: The city's loyalty can be bought.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. It won't blow your mind, but it won't waste your time either.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Mafia III is exactly what it needs to be.
