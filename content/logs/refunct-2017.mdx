## The Experience

Refunct on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> OPERATOR LOG: What makes you smile.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "What makes you smile" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Refunct does its job without distinction.
