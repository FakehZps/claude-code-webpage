## The Setup

Crysis 2 on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> SYSTEM LOG: Entry filed. Crysis 2 — mission complete.

## The Gunplay

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Crysis 2 does its job without distinction.
