## The Experience

Jusant on Steam Deck (Xbox Game Pass) is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> ALTITUDE: 4,200m. Calliaphone resonating with the tower.

## The Detail

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "Climbing then space whales" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. Jusant is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
