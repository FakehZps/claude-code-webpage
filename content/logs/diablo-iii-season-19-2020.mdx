## The Experience

Diablo III : Season 19 on Switch sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> PRIME EVIL DETECTED: Nephalem, proceed to the Rift. Stay a while.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Stay a while and listen" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Diablo III : Season 19 does its job without distinction.
