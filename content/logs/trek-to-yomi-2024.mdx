## The Experience

Trek to Yomi on Nintendo Switch sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> BUSHIDO CODE: Death before dishonour. Hiroki advances.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Old Samurai Movies" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Trek to Yomi does its job without distinction.
