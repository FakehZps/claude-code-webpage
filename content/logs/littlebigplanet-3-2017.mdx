## The Experience

LittleBigPlanet 3 on PS4 is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> OPERATOR LOG: World of imagination.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "World of imagination" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. LittleBigPlanet 3 is exactly what it needs to be.
