## The Experience

Back to the Future: The Game on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> FLUX CAPACITOR: 1.21 gigawatts. Roads? Where we're going we don't need roads.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "I think he took his wallet" says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. Back to the Future: The Game is exactly what it needs to be.
