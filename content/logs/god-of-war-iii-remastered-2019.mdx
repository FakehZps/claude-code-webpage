## The World

God of War III Remastered on PS4 (IGC) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> SPARTAN RAGE: Charging. Atreus, stay close.

## The Combat

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. Completable and forgettable in equal measure.

## Final Verdict

Fine. God of War III Remastered does its job without distinction.
