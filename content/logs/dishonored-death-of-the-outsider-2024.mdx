## The Experience

Dishonored: Death of the Outsider on PC (Xbox Game Pass) sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

> CHAOS LEVEL: Low. Corvo moves like a ghost.

## The Detail

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Black Eyed Bastard" is about right as a summary.

## Final Verdict

Fine. Dishonored: Death of the Outsider does its job without distinction.
