## The Experience

The Sexy Brutale on PC sits comfortably in the "perfectly serviceable" bracket. It works. It's competent. It has moments. None of them will stick with you for long, but you won't regret the time spent.

The edges are rough and the ambition often outstrips the execution, but there's enough here to see it through. "Sam Elliot?" is about right as a summary.

Fine. The Sexy Brutale does its job without distinction.
