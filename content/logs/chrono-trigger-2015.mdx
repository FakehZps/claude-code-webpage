## The Story

Chrono Trigger lands on DS with enough confidence to leave a real impression. There's a clear vision at work here — the kind of game that knows exactly what it wants to be and executes on it almost flawlessly.

> TIME GATE OPENED: Epoch ready. Lavos cannot win.

## The System

The highs are consistently high. A few rough edges keep it from perfection, but they're easy to overlook when the core experience is this satisfying. Summed up well by: "dated but still worthwhile".

## Final Verdict

Highly recommended. Chrono Trigger doesn't waste your time.
