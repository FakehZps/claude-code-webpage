## The Feel

LEGO Harry Potter Collection on Playstation 4 is a decent experience that never quite reaches its potential. There's a good game in here, and it shows through often enough to make the whole thing worthwhile.

> STUD COUNTER: x4 multiplier active. Collect everything.

## The Design

The best moments are genuinely enjoyable. The worst are just... fine. The balance tips positive, but only just. "stabbed by a fish" — that about covers it.

## Final Verdict

Good but flawed. LEGO Harry Potter Collection is worth your time if you know what you're signing up for.
