## The Experience

This War of Mine on PC is a confident, well-made game that delivers on what it promises. Not reinventing anything, but doing its thing with enough craft and care to make the time spent worthwhile.

> SHELTER STATUS: Food critical. Morale broken.

## The Detail

There's a lot to like here. The fundamentals are solid and the experience holds together well from start to finish. The note "In modern war…you will die like a dog for no good reason." says it all, really.

## Final Verdict

Solid. This War of Mine is exactly what it needs to be.
