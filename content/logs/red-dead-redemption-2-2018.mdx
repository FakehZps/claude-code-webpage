## The Experience

Red Dead Redemption 2 is the kind of game that doesn't let go. Picked it up on PS4 and found myself completely absorbed — the kind of experience that makes you lose track of time and reminds you why you play games at all.

> WANTED LEVEL: Increasing. Honour degraded.

## The Detail

Everything here fires on all cylinders. The mechanics are tight, the world rewards exploration, and there's a confidence to the design that keeps pushing you forward. Every system supports every other system.

## Final Verdict

One of the best in its genre. Red Dead Redemption 2 earns its reputation. Play it.
